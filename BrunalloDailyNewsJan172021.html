Brunallo Daily News Sunday January. 17. 2021

Brunallosian President announces 2 week lockdown

Yesterday, the Brunallosian President made a speech about what he will be doing to keep his
country safe. In his speech, he said that because of the rising case numbers, he has decided to put the
whole country in to lockdown starting at 12:01am on Monday January 18. The lockdown will continue until
the first of February. All non-essential businesses will be closed but schools will remain open.

Brunallo transport companies reducing frequency

Due to the new lockdown measures, all of the Brunallo transport companies: Brunallo Airlines, Brunallo
Trains, Brunallo Ferrys and Brunallo Buses all said that because of the new lockdown and 567 more
new COVID-19 cases today, people will not want to travel so much. The lockdown is allowing essential
travel but non-essential travel will not be permitted more than 100kms away from your home to prevent
the spread of COVID-19. Because of this, the travel companies are reducing their frequency.

Hilton hotel in Brunallo closing down

The Hilton hotel which is near the Brunallosian mountains has announced that it will close down in July 2021. Even
before the pandemic, the hotel was not so full says the hotel manager. Now because of the pandemic,
we had around 10% occupancy compared to around 50% in the other Brunallosian hotels. Even last year we had an
average occupancy rate of 60% compared to 85% at the other hotels. This hotel is a bit out of the way and hard
to get there in the mountains being a 3 hour drive from the Brunallosian capital. It was in a beautiful place but
it is hard to get there and not many people go there. Hopefully more people will visit that area soon.
