Brunallo Daily News Thursday February 11, 2021

Pleasure travellers going to France drop by 60%

Brunallo Airlines CEO said that due to the many quarantine rules for travellers going to France, the
number of pleasure travellers going to France has dropped by 60 percent. Brunallo Airlines has been
talking to the Brunallosian President that they cannot make lots of money with these rules. These rules
are currently doing their job because 43 people who where coming from France to Brunallo and were
in their quarantine tested positive for COVID-19 today. A few months ago when there were no rules for
people going to France, that was the most popular destination for pleasure. The rules are set to end on Feb. 23rd.

Brunallo Trains starting maintnance next week

Brunallo Trains said today that on Tuesday, they will start some maintnance on their train tracks. They need
to replace some of their tracks in the mountains. This job will take from Tuesday to Friday and one kilometer
of track will be replaced. Brunallo trains will also be doing maintnance in one of their tunells. Due to these
constructions, there will be 50% less trains from Tuesday to Friday. This is a good time to do the maintnance because
due to COVID-19, not so many people are travelling so not many people will be affected by the reduction of train frequency.

Family day is happening this Monday

On Monday, it will be family day in Brunallo and other countries in the world. Family day is a day where you
stay with your familly and enjoy yourself. School will be closed and most workplaces closed. Due to the lockdown,
there will not be so many celebrations as last year. Still, there will be a parade and several fun activities in all
of the four major Brunallosian cities. To participate in these activities, you need to wear a face covering and you need
to practise physical distancing with each other. The Brunallosian President wishes you a very happy Family Day on Monday.
