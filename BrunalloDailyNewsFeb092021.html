Brunallo Daily News Tuesday February 9th 2021

New rule makes it even harder to travel to France

This morning, a new quarantine rule entered into place. This rule says that all Brunallosians coming
to France have to quarantine for 4 days when arriving in France and monitor for COVID-19 symptoms. If
you do not have any symptoms after 4 days, you are good to go. If you have symptoms, you need to get
tested. This makes very hard to travel to France now that there are 2 rules, one for Brunallosians going
to France and one for Brunallosians coming from France to Brunallo. The 2 rules are the same rule
that was explained above. These rules will currently be in place until Tuesday the 23rd of February

Travel companies reduce frequency to French routes

The Brunallosian travel companies said today that because of even more restrictions for travelers going
to France, they will reduce frequency even more on some of their routes to France. Now with these extra rules,
even less people are travelling so they need to reduce the frequency more. The travel companies are still losing
lots of money. 5 months ago, Brunallo Airlines had three flights a day to Paris, France. Now they have only one
per day. The travel companies hope that the restrictions will end soon so that they can make more money which they need.

Expedia puts Brunallo in top 50 countries to visit

This week, Expedia, the travel booking company said that soon, they will publish their yearly list of the top
50 countries in the world to visit. Today, this list got published and on number 37, it is Brunallo!! This is
the first time that Brunallo got in to the top 40. This is good news for the travel companies because if these
restrictions are permitting, there might be lots of tourists coming to Brunallo. In the list, it said that Brunallo is a
lovely country with nice beaches, beautiful towns, nice mountains and more. I hope more tourists will come this year.
