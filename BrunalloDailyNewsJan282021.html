Brunallo Daily News Thursday January 28. 2021

New quarantine rule stoped hundreds of possible cases

The Brunallosian health department said that this new quarantine rule stoped hundreds
and maybe even thousands of possible COVID-19 cases. Today, 67 travellers coming from France
who where in their quarantine tested positive today. If it was not for this quarantine rule, many
people could have been infected and that number is between the hundreds and thousands. Today,
the Brunallosian president said that a surprisingly big number of people who come from France bring
COVID-19 with them to. He also said that for safety reasons, this quarantine rule will remain in place until February 9th.

Brunallo Airlines suspending routes to Canada

Brunallo Airlines CEO said today that due to new travel restrictions in Canada, Brunallo Airlines
will suspend all routes to Canada indefinitely. The new travel restrictions say that when you arrive in
Canada, you will be tested for COVID in a hotel that you pay for (the test takes a few days so it would
cost around 1000$). If you test negative, then you are free to go, if not you have to quarantine for 14 in a
hotel that you payed for. Currently, they have 1 flight a week to Toronto from Brunallo and that will be suspended.

Brunallo travel companies cutting routes

Today, Brunallo Trains and Brunallo Ferrys said that they would all cut some frequency of their
routes. They are doing this because of the new rule that got put in place on Tuesday. This rule says that
all travellers coming from France have to quarantine for 4 days when they come to Brunallo. Before, there
was no quarantine rule for people coming from France so it was the travel companies most profitable route in
2020. The travel companies will cut around 50% of their frequency to France until the rule ends on February 9th.
