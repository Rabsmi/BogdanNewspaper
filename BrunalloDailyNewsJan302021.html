Brunallo Daily News Saturday January 30. 2021

Major snowfall has major impacts on train schedules

Last night, there was a major snowfall in the Brunallosian mountains. Between 10-20
centimetres of snow fell in the mountains. That means that the train tracks in the mountains
are covered with snow. Because of this, Brunallo trains has suspended all train operations today.
The cleaning of the tracks is expected to take all day long. Brunallo Trains will be providing bus
transportation to people who want it when the roads are drivable. The roads are expected to be
clean of snow by 2pm. Brunallo Trains is apologizing for this and says that they are working hard to clean the tracks.

Brunallosian President making speech today

Today, at 5pm, the Brunallosian President will make a speech. In this speech, he will announce
if the lockdown continues in Brunallo. There is a chance that lockdown will continue because case
numbers are still around the same and this lockdown did not do anything to reduce the number of cases.
If the lockdown continues, all non-essential businesses will be closed, most schools will remain open and
non-essential travel will be permitted within 100km of your home. Go to BR news at 5pm local time today.

Over 60 year olds eligible for COVID-19 vaccine

Today, the Brunallosian Health Department said that anyone over 60 years old can sign up on the
list to get the COVID-19 vaccine. This is because the health department also said that almost all people
over 80 years old who signed up for the COVID-19 vaccine already got it and all people over 80 should
be vaccinated by the 3rd of February. This is why the government is letting all people over 60 years old to
get on the waiting list for the COVID-19 vaccine. To sign up to the vaccine list, just go to your local doctor.
