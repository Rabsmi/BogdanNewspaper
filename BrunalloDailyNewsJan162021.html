Brunallo Daily News Saturday January 16. 2021

572 new COVID-19 cases in Brunallo today

Today, the Brunallosian health department reported 572 new COVID-19 cases. That number
is the biggest in the last 10 days and that is not a good sign. Also, today the Brunallosian President
will make a speech at 4:00pm local to announce what safety measures should be put in place to make the country safe.

Brunallosian President making speech

Today at 4:00pm local time, the Brunallosian President will make a speech announcing what
he will do to ensure the country is safe. Today, the Brunallosian health department reported 572 new
COVID-19 cases which is the highest in the last 10 days. Because of this, the President might decide
to put the country in lockdown. The numbers are still rising. Wait until 4pm to see what will happen
to Brunallo. Do not forget to go to BR news at 4pm today to listen to the President making his speech.

Brunallo Ferry company adding more frequency

The Brunallosian ferry company, Brunallo Ferrys, announced today that starting February first, restrictions
permitting of course, it will increase the frequency of ferrys on their busiest route. The company says that
ferrys on their buisiest route, the Brunallosian capital to Nice, France are 80% full on that route and that more
people are starting to travel so instead of a ferry every two days, they will put a ferry a day. This is a good sign that
people are restarting to travel. The company also said that they might increase frequency on other routes soon enough.
This is a good sign that people are starting to travel again and that life is slowly getting back to normal.
