Brunallo Daily News Saturday February 1st 2021

Brunallosian President announces more lockdown

Yesterday, the Brunallosian President made a speech. In that speech, he announced that
the lockdown that was supposed to end today will be continuing for 2 more weeks until the
15th of February. The President said that this lockdown will continue because case numbers
are around the same. When we started this lockdown we had 565 new COVID-19 cases, today, there
are 553 new cases. All non-essential business will be closed. Also before there was a rule that allowed
non-essential travel within 100 kilometres of your home, now it is only 50 kilometres. This is to stop COVID-19.

Some schools still remain closed

Today, the main school board in the Brunallosian capital, Capital school board says that all of their
schools will remain closed during this lockdown. The school board said that they are continuing to
close schools because all of their schools are in the Brunallosian capital and that is where 70% of all
COVID-19 cases are. For example today, 412 out of the 573 cases in the country are in the capital. The
schools where already closed since the start of the lockdown and if the lockdown continues, the schools will be closed.

Some Brunallosian ski hills closing

Some Brunallosian ski hills have announced that they will close temporarily due to this lockdown.
In the last 2 weeks you where allowed non-essential travel within 100 kilometres of your home. Now
with the new lockdown rules that started yesterday, non-essential travel will only be permitted within
50 kilometres of your home. This is why some of the Brunallosian ski hills are closing. Some of the ski
hills are not 50 kilometres away from a major city so nobody will come to them. That is why they are closing.
