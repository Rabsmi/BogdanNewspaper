Brunallo Daily News Wednesday January. 20. 2021

Brunallosian Persident considering new quarantine rule

Today out of the 586 new COVID-19 cases, which was the highest number in the last two
weeks, 46 of those cases where from people who flew in from France between the 13 and 15th of
January. There is no quarantine rule for France because France and Brunallo are like brothers and
sisters. The people who got infected, infected other people who infected others and so on. That is why
the President is considering to impose a 10 day quarantine for all people coming from France.

Travel companies against possible new quarantine rule

The Brunallosian travel companies are against a possible new quarantine rule which would make people
coming from France quarantine for 10 days when entering Brunallo. Currently there is no rule. The travel companies
Brunallo Ferrys and Brunallo Airlines are against this new rule because now their most profitable routes are to France.
This is because people coming from France do not need to quarantine when they get back. If the rule gets put into
place, the travel companies will have to cut a lot of frequency. The President will announce if the rule gets in place or not on Saturday.

Brunallo Trains not affected by lockdown

The Brunallosian train company, Brunallo Trains has not and will not be affected by this lockdown. This is because
people are allowed to travel even in lockdown. That is because this lockdown allows non-essential travel within 100 kilometres
of your home. Many people will take a short train ride during the weekend to the mountains, beautiful old villages or to
go to the beach. Brunallo Trains is happy with this rule and urges people to come on their trains for a little weekend
trip. Trains are sanitized after every journey and a face covering is required. Brunallo Trains may increase the frequency on
some of their most popular routes. Also, capacity is limited to 75% the normal capacity for safety reasons.
