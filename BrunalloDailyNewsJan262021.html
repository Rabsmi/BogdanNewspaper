Brunallo Daily News Tuesday January 26. 2021

New quarantine rule starts today

Today, a new quarantine rule will be put in place. On Saturday, the President announced
a new quarantine rule. This rule says that everybody coming from France will have to quarantine for
4 days when they get back and monitor to see if they have symptoms. If they do not, you are free
to go. If you do have symptoms, you need to be tested. The Brunallosian government says that they
will monitor to make sure that people respect this rule and if you do not respect it, you will have to pay
half a million euros for the fine. The government hopes that this rule will help stop the spread of COVID-19.

Brunallo Trains talking about a new tunnel

Brunallo Trains CEO has announced today that his company are in talks to build a new tunnel
through the Brunallosian mountains. The tunnel would be around 30 kilometres long. There are
many good reasons to building this tunnel. Firstly, it would save lots of time because currently, the
track goes through difficult terrain. For trains which do not stop at the stations in the mountains, it would
save them 30 minutes. The company will talk to see if they want to build it and then the government has to approve it.

Brunallosian airport has huge drop in passengers

Today, the only Brunallosian airport which is located near the capital reported that it had huge drops in
passenger numbers. In 2019, the airport CEO said that they had around 10 million passengers, in 2020
those numbers are around 3 million passengers. This is a 70% decrease in passenger numbers. The airport
is hopeful that 2021 numbers will be a bit better but quarantine rules are making travel a lot harder. Now
with the vaccine coming to Brunallo, it might be a bit easier to travel in 2021. The airport has lost a lot of
money and they hope that over 5 million people will travel through the airport in 2021. They hope around 10 million in 2023.
