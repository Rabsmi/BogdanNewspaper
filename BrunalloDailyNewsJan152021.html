Brunallo Daily News Friday January 15. 2021

563 new COVID-19 cases in Brunallo today

We reported 563 new cases today, the highest number in the last week. The president of Brunallo
says that this is bad and that the country might, consider new measures or even lockdown to ensure
the country's safety. The president will make a speech on Saturday an 4:00pm local time to adress
the citizents on what should be done to ensure the the country's safety.

Brunallo Airlines seeing huge losses

Today, the CEO of Brunallo Arilines said that they have had a 4.2bn$ loss during the 2020 year.
This is because of the COVID pandemic. The airline said that they have never in their 74 year history
had such losses and such low occupancy rates. The airline cut 90% of their flights and also,
they had a 63% occupancy rate average compared to 2019 with 85%. The airline says many people are still
scared to fly. The airline encourages people to fly but the quarantine rules are making travel very difficult.

Brunallo mask factory expanding due to huge demand

The mask factory outsite of the Bunallosian capital which was built at the start of the pandemic to cope
with the immense increase in the demand of masks has anounced that it will do an expansion. The mask company
CEO stated that his company are making huge profits and that there is a huge demand in masks that they cannot
keep up to, so he anounced that his company will expand their factory by 10%. The factory is capable of producing
10000 single use masks a day or 7 a minute and after the expansion, they will be able to do 11000 masks a day. The
CEO said this is a huge step towards helping our country fight against COVID-19.
