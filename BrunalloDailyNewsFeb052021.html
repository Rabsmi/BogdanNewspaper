Brunallo Daily News Friday February 5th 2021

Brunallosian wind storm delays aircraft

This morning, a big wind storm was blowing through the Brunallosian capital. There where
winds of 50 kilometres per hour gusting up to 80 kilometres per hour. Worst of all, at the airport
near the Brunallosian capital, those winds where crosswinds. This made it hard and almost impossible
to land of take off an aircraft. Due to that, 80% of flights arriving or departing got delayed. Some
of the arriving flights diverted to other airports due to the wind. Currently, the winds are lighter and
aircraft are departing and arriving as normal. Most flights are still delayed between 30 and 60 minutes.

Brunallosian President and French President had a meeting

Today, the Brunallosain President spoke with the French President Emmanuel Macron. Macron
wanted people coming from Brunallo to France to quarantine when arriving from Brunallo. This is
because today, 56 people who arrived in France from Brunallo tested positive for COVID-19 today. The
French President would like a 4 day quarantine rule for people coming in France to Brunallo. In this rule,
you would check for symptoms during 4 days. If you do not have symptoms, your are good, if you do, you will be tested.

New quarantine rule may be put in place

Today, the Brunallosian President and the French President talked about a possible new quarantine
rule for Brunallosians coming in to France. If the rule gets put in place, it is the same as if you would be
coming from France to Brunallo. Tomorrow the two Presidents will decide if the want the rule to be put
in place. On Monday, there will be a speech and the Presidents will announce of there is a new quarantine
rule. Also, the Brunallosian President will announce on Monday if the lockdown will end this Tuesday or not.
